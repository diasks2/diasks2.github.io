Feature alignment document

This is our Feature Alignment Document at Ambiki which we updated and simplified at the start of this year when we switched to our new product development process.

We emphasize breaking projects into smaller parts, with the maximum duration for a subproject set at two weeks. Each subproject is accompanied by a Feature Alignment Document.

These Feature Alignment Documents are drafted prior to the warm-up week.

Some insights from this initial cycle include:

  • The overview videos were highly successful, providing developers with additional context.
  • Begin writing the document by addressing the problem.
  • Avoid merging the solution with your problem statement.
  • Do not overlook terminology - it can be crucial.
  • This document has proven extremely beneficial for our QA team.
