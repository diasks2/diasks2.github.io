3 uniques

Sue Melone recently recommended the book "Traction" by Gino Wickman to me. While reading, I got sidetracked on page 10; the "Organizational Checkup." One of the checklist items delved into the concept of "Our 3 Uniques™ (differentiators)".

While I had a general notion of what these might be for our organization, I realized we had never formally articulated them. So, Bailey Morgan, M.A., CCC-SLP, Bethany Ayer MS, OTR/L, MBA and I took a deep dive. Here are our current three differentiators:

  1. Holistic Team: Our clinicians know technology; our developers understand healthcare. A combination that ensures a product deeply attuned to therapists' needs.
  2. Cutting-Edge & Clean UX: With exclusive features such as AI tools and Tenalog®, Ambiki's sleek interface and simple user experience outshine traditional EMRs.
  3. Tailored for SLPs, OTs, and PTs: Ambiki is meticulously designed for the specific nuances and unique challenges of SLP, OT, and PT practice management.
