SaaS-land

On the faraway hill of SaaS-land, they say,
A platform was built, bright as the day.
It started for speech, to help those in need,
But soon shifted focus, all driven by greed.

Mental health, they shouted, is where money's at!
So they turned their back on the speech folks—just like that!
The speech therapists clamored, "What about us?"
But the platform just grew, without any fuss.

Ignored and forgotten, their voices grew small,
As the app kept on rising, to profit’s big call.
But down on the ground, in the speech therapist crowd,
A company named Ambiki stood tall and proud.

"We hear you," they said, "and we understand,
Your work is important, your needs must be planned!"
With care and precision, they built it anew,
Focused on speech, and on helping YOU!

So, the moral, dear friends, is crystal and clear,
Ambiki is here, with nothing to fear.
They won't stray from the path, they know what's true—
Supporting speech therapists, and supporting YOU!
