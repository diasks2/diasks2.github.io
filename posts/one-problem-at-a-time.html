One problem at a time

The number one issue I see when people get stuck is trying to solve multiple problems at once, often without realizing they've bundled together several issues.

  • Solve one problem at a time
  • Break that problem down into smaller, manageable segments
  • Build the wall brick by brick

Stumbling blocks often arise not from the complexity of the problem itself, but from skipping foundational steps. A clear definition of the problem and a methodical approach to tackling it piece by piece can help unlock the gates to efficiency.
